Deadly Premonition 2: A Blessing in Disguise

60

The player controls one of the two protagonists investigating the town. The town is an open area which York navigates using a skateboard. The player talks to the various characters which opens up tasks after some dialogue. Completing these tasks moves along the plot. There are some sequences that act like a minigame such as York driving a Jet Ski.
