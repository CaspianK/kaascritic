No Straight Roads

64

The game takes place in the fictional high-tech metropolis of Vinyl City, which is stated to be the music capital of the world. The city is governed by No Straight Roads (NSR), a corrupt electronic music label whose goal is to monopolize the music industry and continue their control of the city and maintain order. Vinyl City is made up of several districts.
